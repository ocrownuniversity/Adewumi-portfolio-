About me

As a product designer, i prioritize usability and user experience while creating an aesthetically pleasing product. While being involved in all user experience design processes that cover research, prototyping, and iterations, I also specialize in collaborating with product leads to develop product strategy and drive product vision.

In my previous roles, I have been responsible for managing design processes that have improved collaboration within the entire product development team. I have so far built products across several industries: SAAS, Fintech, education, e-commerce, health, transportation and logistics, real estate, and consumer tech.
I am always striving to grow and to keep learning wherever I find the opportunity to add and showcase my valuable skills.
